RFP-001: Fiber Optic Cable Supply

Submission Due Date: 2025-02-20

The Department of Telecommunications invites bids for the supply of 50 km of Single Mode Fiber Optic Cables (OS2, ITU-T G.652D). The scope includes cable supply, factory testing, type tests, and delivery to the central warehouse.

Scope of Supply

  • Single Mode Fiber Cable – 50 km
  • Outdoor Armored Cable – 24 Core
  • Factory Acceptance Testing
  • Type Approval Tests
