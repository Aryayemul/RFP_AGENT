RFP-002: LV & MV Power Cable Procurement

Submission Due Date: 2025-03-15

The State Electricity Board requests proposals for the procurement of Low Voltage (LV) and Medium Voltage (MV) power cables. The vendor must comply with IEC 60502-1 and IEC 60502-2 standards. Testing requirements include HV withstand tests, insulation resistance, and conductor resistance tests.

Scope of Supply

  • LV 1.1 kV Aluminum Cable – 3C x 240 sqmm – 10 km
  • MV 11 kV Copper Cable – 3C x 95 sqmm – 5 km
  • Routine Tests
  • Type Tests
